The History of TechCon

Tech-On was founded in 2010 with a vision to unite technology enthusiasts and innovators in exploring the frontiers of tech. By 2015, Tech-On had expanded internationally, bringing together thousands from around the world to dive into the latest in AI, cybersecurity, and more.

Evolution of Tech

Since its inception, TechCon has grown into a global event, attracting thousands of attendees annually.

Our Mission

TechCon is driven by a mission to empower the global tech community through collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing.

Our speakers

Each year, the conference features a diverse lineup of renowned speakers, including visionary entrepreneurs, cutting-edge researchers, and industry leaders.
